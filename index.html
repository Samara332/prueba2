It is a web application visible on mobile and desktop devices that allows you to take a picture of mathematical expressions that you do not know, giving you information about this and other topics as well as teaching material, tutorials and recommended books.

 

ü™êHow does it work?

Using convolutional neural networks, a model capable of identifying different mathematical symbols was trained, through some lines of html, css and js, as well as the cell phone camera, we can scan the mathematical expressions that are unknown to us. Also when creating an account our data and preferences are stored for later use.

   

ü™êWhy use FIMATH?

A very common problem when trying to learn mathematics is not knowing where to start, the equations presented in the books are threatening and discourage the student. With Fimath that problem is in the past, just by scanning the equation, Fimath shows you what type of equation it is, relevant information, resources to learn how to solve it, as well as previous topics that are required for its full understanding, in a fast and fun way.
